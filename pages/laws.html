Improving environmental principles to shape better laws

Harmonizing Environment, Economy, and Society

"(a) the priority of environmental protection goals and activities within the framework of achieving the economic and social-human interests of the population for the present and the future;"

Aspects Overlooked:

  1. Implementation Mechanisms for Holistic Sustainability
  2. Balancing Conflicting Priorities
  3. Ensuring Inclusive Decision-Making Processes

Our Enhancement Suggestions:
  1. Develop clear criteria and methodologies for balancing environmental, economic, and social interests.
  2. Establish robust governance structures to address conflicts and ensure equitable outcomes.
  3. Prioritize community engagement and participatory approaches in policy formulation.

Enforcement of Environmental Regulations

"(b) the obligation to enforce the legislation on environmental protection, compliance with standards, regulations and permissible limits on the use of natural resources and energy, on the application of chemical, physical and biological factors on environmental components, on harmful emissions and discharges, on the disposal of waste from economic activities;"

Aspects Overlooked:

  1. Adequate resources and mechanisms for effective enforcement.
  2. Addressing loopholes or inconsistencies in existing regulations.
  3. Ensuring accountability and transparency in enforcement actions.

Our Enhancement Suggestions:
  1. Strengthen regulatory agencies with sufficient funding, personnel, and technological resources.
  2. Regular review and update of regulations to adapt to evolving environmental challenges.
  3. Establishing penalties and sanctions for non-compliance to deter violations.

Environmental Accountability and Remediation

"(c) the responsibility of all natural and legal persons for damage caused to the environment; the prevention, limitation and combating of pollution, as well as the recovery of damage caused to the environment and its components on behalf of natural and legal persons who have admitted (even unconsciously or negligently) the damage;"

Aspects Overlooked:

  1. Clear mechanisms for determining responsibility and liability for environmental damage.
  2. Strategies for prevention and mitigation of pollution at its source.
  3. Effective methods for environmental restoration and remediation.

Our Enhancement Suggestions:
  1. Establishing comprehensive frameworks for assigning responsibility and liability for environmental harm, including both natural and legal persons.
  2. Implementing stringent pollution prevention measures, such as pollution control technologies and sustainable practices.
  3. Facilitating collaboration between stakeholders, including governments, businesses, and communities, to address environmental challenges collectively.

Environmental Responsibility in Resource Ownership

"(d) The transfer of the right of ownership and use of natural resources to natural and legal persons does not exempt the beneficiaries of these rights from compliance with environmental protection legislation;"

Aspects Overlooked:

  1. Ensuring that ownership and usage rights of natural resources align with environmental protection goals.
  2. Addressing potential conflicts between resource exploitation and environmental conservation.
  3. Providing incentives and support for sustainable resource management practices.

Our Enhancement Suggestions:
  1. Enforcing strict adherence to environmental protection legislation for all parties involved in the ownership and use of natural resources.
  2. Implementing comprehensive environmental impact assessments for resource development projects to mitigate potential environmental harm.
  3. Encouraging the adoption of sustainable resource management practices through regulatory incentives and capacity-building initiatives.

State Oversight of Environmental Compliance

"(e) The State Environmental Inspectorate has the right to request written explanations from natural and legal persons for each case of violation by them of environmental protection legislation."

Aspects Overlooked:

  1. Ensuring effective enforcement of environmental protection legislation by the State Environmental Inspectorate.
  2. Providing clarity on the procedures and requirements for responding to requests for written explanations.
  3. Addressing potential challenges or limitations in the enforcement process.

Our Enhancement Suggestions:
  1. Strengthening the authority and capacity of the State Environmental Inspectorate to enforce environmental laws and regulations.
  2. Establishing clear guidelines and protocols for requesting written explanations from individuals and entities found in violation of environmental legislation.
  3. Facilitating cooperation and information-sharing between the State Environmental Inspectorate and other relevant agencies to enhance enforcement effectiveness and coordination.
